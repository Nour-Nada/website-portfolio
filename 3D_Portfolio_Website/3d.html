3D

Running Robot

A short 3D running sequence made for pwnisher's Kinetic Rush community render challenge. The entire animation was created in Blender and conceived by me. Due to hardware limitations the render is a bit flickry and some imperfections could not be fixed. Color grading was done in DaVinci Resolve. Assets were used from KitBash3d and other online sources

Tranquil Gardens

A realistic 3D render made in Blender attempting to capture the tranquility of lush green gardens. For final touchups and color grading DaVinci Resolve was used. In this render I utilized vegetation addons and photoscans.

Summer Tranquility

A realistic 3D render made in Blender, capturing the warm, nostalgic feel of summer with a vintage film look. Final touchups and color grading were done in DaVinci Resolve to complete the look. The scene uses premade assets from the BlenderKit add-on.

Lost At Sea

A Black and White Realistic 3D render made in Blender of a floating buoy lost at sea. The black and white effect was achieved through changing the view transform and then doing some final touchups and color grading done in DaVinci Resolve to complete the look. This scene uses a premade asset.

Hidden City

A stylized render made in Blender for the "Secrets of the Luminara" challenge run by KitBash3d. The stylized effects and color grading are done in Photoshop along with some touchups. Assets from KitBash3d and vegetation addons were used in the making of this scene.

Camera Visualization

A lifelike 3D product render of a Canon camera created in Blender. Final touchups and color grading were done in DaVinci Resolve. The scene incorporates assets and photogrammetry elements.

Lotion Visualization

A lifelike 3D product visualization of a Lotion Bottle created in Blender. Final touchups and color grading were done in DaVinci Resolve. This scene is fully modeled and textured by me besides the vegetation.

Hidden City

A old style render made in Blender for the "Secrets of the Luminara" challenge run by KitBash3d. The timey filter effects and color grading are done in DaVinci Resolve along with some touchups.
